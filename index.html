Rules
  1. Each active player gets four cards from a 32-card deck. Two-player online rooms may choose eight cards.
  2. Card power is 10, 9, 8, 7, A, K, Q, J. There is no trump.
  3. Dirty Laundry can exchange a weak hand: four A/K/Q/J cards, or three of those plus one 7.
  4. Players must follow the led suit when possible.
  5. Only the final trick decides the round winner.
  6. Toep raises the stake by one. Others may join or step out; no counter-Toep until play moves.
  7. Folding pays the previous stake immediately.
  8. Active round losers pay the current stake. Players at zero lives leave the table.
