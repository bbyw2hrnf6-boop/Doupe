Rules
  1. Toepen: four cards each, or eight in two-player duel rooms. Power is 10, 9, 8, 7, A, K, Q, J.
  2. Toepen: follow suit when possible. Only the final trick decides the round winner.
  3. Toepen: Dirty Laundry can be claimed once per player per round before play starts.
  4. Toepen: only the trick leader may Toep, and only before leading that trick.
  5. Schnautz / 31: each player gets three cards. Three cards lie open in the center.
  6. Schnautz / 31: score the best same-suit sum. A is 11; 10, K, Q, J are 10; number cards keep value.
  7. Schnautz / 31: three equal ranks score 30.5. A suited 31 is strongest and can end the round at once.
  8. Schnautz / 31: on your turn, swap one card with the center, swap all three, pass, or knock.
  9. Schnautz / 31: after a knock, every other active player gets one final turn.
  10. Schnautz / 31: lowest score loses a life. At zero lives you swim; lose again and you are out.
