Rules
  1. Each active player gets four cards from a 32-card deck.
  2. Card power is 10, 9, 8, 7, A, K, Q, J. There is no trump.
  3. Dirty Laundry can exchange a weak hand: four A/K/Q/J cards, or three of those plus one 7.
  4. Players must follow the led suit when possible.
  5. Only the fourth trick decides the round winner.
  6. Toep raises the stake by one. Folding pays the previous stake immediately.
  7. Active round losers pay the current stake. Players at zero lives leave the table.
